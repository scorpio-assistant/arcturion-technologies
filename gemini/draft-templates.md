CTA: Subscribe + GitHub star
Cadence: Weekly (Sundays 2:00 PM EDT)

### **Authority Threads on X/Twitter**
```yaml
Pattern ID: x-authority-thread
Platforms: Twitter/X, LinkedIn
Visual Style: Text-only or simple charts
Caption Structure: Thread format (7-15 tweets)
CTA: Follow + newsletter signup
Cadence: Bi-weekly (Tuesdays & Fridays 10:00 AM EDT)
Risk Level: Medium (public claims)
Monetization: Newsletter signups, follower growth
Auto-Publish Eligibility: After 5 consecutive approvals
```

### **Reddit Trend Commentary**
```yaml
Pattern ID: reddit-trend-analysis
Platforms: Reddit (r/MachineLearning, r/artificial, r/singularity)
Visual Style: Screenshots + markdown analysis
Caption Structure: Trend identification + technical analysis + implications
CTA: Upvote + comment discussion
Cadence: When trending topics emerge
Risk Level: High (community backlash)
Monetization: Karma farming, community credibility
Auto-Publish Eligibility: NEVER (manual approval always)
```
